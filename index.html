About Custom DTF Transfers

If you are in Jacksonville and looking for a way to create amazing custom t-shirts, hats, or bags, you have found the right place. For those seeking top quality and reliable service, finding a trusted provider for Custom DTF Transfers Jacksonville is the first step to bringing your designs to life.

DTF stands for "Direct-to-Film." It is a modern way to print designs onto fabric. Think of it like a high-tech sticker for your clothes. First, a printer puts your design onto a special clear film. Then, a layer of adhesive powder is added. Finally, you use a heat press to stick that design permanently onto a t-shirt or any other fabric.

The result is a design that is bright, soft to the touch, and very durable. It can handle many washes without cracking or fading. This method works on almost any color or type of fabric, which makes it a very flexible option for everyone.

For a long time, people used methods like screen printing or vinyl heat transfer. These methods are good, but they have limits. Screen printing needs a lot of setup and is best for large orders of the same design. Vinyl can feel thick and plastic-like on the shirt.

DTF printing solves these problems. It is perfect for small orders, even just one shirt. The print feels soft and light, like part of the fabric itself. It can also print very complex designs with many colors and fine details without any trouble. This is why many small businesses and artists are now choosing DTF.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are DTF transfers? +

DTF stands for Direct-to-Film. It's a printing method where designs are printed onto a special film and then transferred to fabric using heat and pressure. The result is a vibrant, durable print that feels soft on the fabric.

What is the minimum I can order? +

We have no minimum order requirements. You can order just one transfer if that's all you need, making it perfect for testing designs or small projects.

What kind of press do I need? +

You'll need a standard heat press with adjustable temperature and pressure settings. Most common heat press models work perfectly with our DTF transfers.

What sizes are available? +

We offer transfers in a wide range of sizes, from small 2-inch designs up to full 16x20 inch prints. Custom sizes are also available upon request.

How do transfers hold up in the wash? +

Our DTF transfers are extremely durable and can withstand 50+ washes with minimal degradation when applied correctly. We recommend washing inside out in cold water for best results.
